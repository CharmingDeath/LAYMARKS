## Include This In Support Requests

To resolve issues quickly, please include:

- app version (for example `0.1.0`)
- platform and OS version (iOS/Android version and device model)
- steps to reproduce the issue
- screenshots or screen recording (if possible)
- relevant error message text

## Common Troubleshooting

### App does not load market data

- Confirm internet connectivity.
- Try again after a short delay.
- Check whether backend/API services are reachable.

### Search returns no results

- Try ticker symbol and company name variations.
- Ensure there are no leading/trailing spaces in the query.

### External article link does not open

- Verify browser availability and network connectivity.
- Retry by reopening the app and selecting the article again.
