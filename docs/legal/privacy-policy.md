# LAYMARKS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-24

LAYMARKS ("we", "our", or "us") provides a market intelligence application and related backend services.

## 1) Information We Collect

### Information you provide

LAYMARKS currently does not require user account registration and does not collect direct personal profile data such as name, address, or date of birth.

### Technical information

When using the app, technical request data may be processed by our backend services and third-party API providers to deliver content, such as:

- device/network request metadata required for API delivery
- requested symbols, search terms, and content queries
- response and error logs for operational reliability

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data through core app flows.

## 2) How We Use Information

We use processed data to:

- deliver quotes, company search results, and financial news content
- operate and secure backend infrastructure
- troubleshoot errors and improve app reliability

## 3) Third-Party Services

LAYMARKS integrates with third-party data providers (for example, market and news APIs). Requests may be transmitted to these providers under their own privacy terms.

You should review each third-party provider policy where applicable.

## 4) Data Retention

Operational logs and cached content may be retained for service reliability, debugging, and abuse prevention for a limited period based on infrastructure needs.

## 5) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers necessary to deliver app functionality (for example, hosting or API providers).

## 6) Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 7) Children's Privacy

LAYMARKS is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8) International Use

If you access LAYMARKS from outside the country where infrastructure is hosted, your information may be processed in other jurisdictions.

## 9) Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data. Contact us to submit a request.

## 10) Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.
